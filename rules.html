Welcome to the Zenon MC Rules Page

Hey everyone! The server owner here! I hope you have a great time and a fun experience on our Minecraft and Discord server. But everything comes with laws, and for that, I give you the laws of our community.

Discord Rules

  1. Do not discuss staff reports/incidents over Discord.
  2. Do not use inappropriate language.
  3. Do not be rude or bully others.
  4. Do not spam or refuse to utilize push-to-talk when necessary.
  5. Do not record others without their permission.
  6. Report Discord issues appropriately.
  7. Do not use bots for the bad reasons.
  8. Be civil and courteous and have fun.

Minecraft Rules

  1. No Griefing
  2. No Spamming
  3. No Advertising
  4. No Cursing/No Constant Cursing
  5. No Trolling/Flaming
  6. No Banned Items (Tekkit Rule)
  7. No Asking for OP, Ranks, or Items
  8. Respect all Players
  9. Obey Staff; They are the Law
  10. No Racist Remarks
  11. No Mods/Hacks
  12. No Full Caps Messages
  13. No Builds Near Spawn
  14. No 1x1 Towers

Mod/Admin Rules (Minecraft)

  1. Be Responsible with the privileges you are given as a Builder/Mod.
  2. Do not spawn blocks or items for other players.
  3. When Trading, only buy and sell legit items.
  4. Only help build for other players using legit items and blocks.
  5. No Power Abuse.
